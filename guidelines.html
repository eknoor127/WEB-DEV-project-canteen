Canteen Guidelines & Policies

A few simple rules to keep things smooth for everyone!

📝 Registration

  • One-time registration is required using your Student ID before placing your first order
  • Use your official college Student ID number (e.g., 25129001)
  • Your phone number is required for order notifications
  • Registration is free and takes less than 30 seconds

🛒 Ordering Rules

  • Select your desired pickup time while placing the order
  • Orders must be placed at least 15 minutes before the desired pickup time
  • Maximum 5 items per order during peak hours (12:30 – 1:00 PM)
  • Special instructions can be added in the notes section
  • Check the menu for estimated preparation times before ordering

⏰ Pickup & Timing

  • Arrive at the counter within 10 minutes of your scheduled pickup time
  • Show your token number (displayed on the order confirmation) at the counter
  • If you're late by more than 15 minutes, your order may be given to walk-in students
  • During peak hours, prefer using the "15-Minute Break Mode" for quick items

💳 Payment Policies

  • Pay at Counter: Cash or UPI accepted at the pickup counter
  • UPI at Pickup: Scan the QR code displayed at the counter
  • Pay Later (No-Penalty): Pay within 4 hours if you forgot your wallet or phone died
  • All payments are tracked automatically — no separate records needed
  • Every payment is mapped to its order for full transparency

🚫 Missed Pickup Policy

  • If you order but do not pick up, you'll receive a warning
  • After 3 missed pickups, a temporary ordering restriction may be applied
  • If you order and don't pay, the amount will be tracked on your student account
  • Cancellations must be done at least 10 minutes before the pickup time
  • In genuine emergencies, contact the canteen staff — we understand!

🎁 Rewards & Coupons

  • First order? Use code SGGSCC50 for a free Samosa!
  • Every 50th order placed through the system is completely FREE
  • Look out for surprise coupons and daily deals on the homepage
  • Refer a friend and both of you get a special discount
  • Frequent orderers may receive loyalty rewards

🤝 Fair Usage Policy

  • Be respectful to canteen staff at all times
  • Do not place fake or troll orders — this affects other students
  • Clean up your table after eating — let's keep our canteen clean!
  • Report any issues using the feedback form on the homepage
  • Share suggestions to improve the system — we're always listening

⚡ 15-Minute Break Mode

  • Toggle "15-Min Break Mode" on the menu page to see only quick items
  • These are items with prep time under 8 minutes
  • Perfect for when you only have a short break between classes
  • Includes snacks, tea, and pre-made items
  • Combine with time scheduling for the fastest experience

📱 Technical Help

  • The website works on all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Clear your browser cache if you face any display issues
  • Your cart is saved in the browser — don't clear cookies while ordering
  • For any technical issues, contact us through the feedback form
